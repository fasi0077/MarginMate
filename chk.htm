EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)

EMI is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. EMIs are used for loans like home loans, car loans, and personal loans.

EMI Calculation in Different Countries

Pakistan: EMI calculations follow a reducing balance method similar to India, with varied interest rates.

India: EMI is calculated using the formula: P × r × (1 + r)^n / ((1 + r)^n - 1)

USA: Uses amortization schedules where interest is higher in early months.

UK: Similar to the US, but many mortgages use interest-only payments initially.
