Understanding EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)

EMI is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. It is used for loans like home loans, car loans, and personal loans.

EMI in Different Countries

  • Pakistan: EMI calculations follow a reducing balance method similar to India, with varied interest rates.
  • India: EMI is calculated using standard banking formulas.
  • USA: Uses amortization schedules where interest is higher in early months.
  • UK: Some mortgages start with interest-only payments.
